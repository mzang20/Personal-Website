My Work

Optical Character Recognition Application (OCR)

During my volunteering at the Smithsonian Library, I was in charge of transcribing historical documents with people's handwriting. This made me think of a more efficient way to do my job. As a result, I constructed a Python-based OCR program that can scan photos and identify characters while subsequently displaying the text on-screen.

Mines Casino Game

In my Fundamental Computing Class, we were tasked to unleash our creativity by combining our C programming skills with graphics libraries to craft our own game. I developed a rendition of a popular casino game which I have always questioned the probablity and statistics. Players interact with a grid, strategically avoiding "bombs" while an internal number system keeps track of their money.
